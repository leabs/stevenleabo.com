Zen Mode

Experience a quick and hassle-free method to compile and share embedded content with Zen Mode. It is completely open-source, ensuring an ad-free and tracking-free environment.

Obtain a comprehensive overview of crucial content such as breaking news, stock market openings, or live train cameras. If you come across something intriguing on any of the streams, easily switch to full screen mode and unmute the video for an enhanced viewing experience.
